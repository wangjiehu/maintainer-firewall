# Code of Conduct

Maintainer Firewall exists to make open-source collaboration calmer and more useful. Be respectful, specific, and patient with contributors and maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep feedback actionable and focused on the work.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, or public shaming.
- Do not post secrets, private vulnerability details, or exploit instructions in public issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that make collaboration unsafe or unproductive. Repeated violations may result in a block from the project.

Report conduct concerns to the repository maintainers through a private channel.
